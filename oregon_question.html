The Oregon Question

Committee Description

Topic

Amidst the rugged landscapes of the American West, a clash of visions and ambitions played out, echoing the thundering hooves of pioneers and the echoes of manifest destiny. It was a time when the spirit of exploration collided with the thirst for land and resources, igniting a powder keg of tensions between settlers and native tribes. This was the Oregon Territorial Dispute when dreams of expansion met the reality of conflicting territorial claims. As delegates, you will explore the complexities of diplomacy and negotiation as the United States and Great Britain wrestled with competing claims, each determined to secure their foothold in the Pacific Northwest.
