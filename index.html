Alberta Model United Nations

First Iteration

February 16th-17th, 2024

Dear prospective delegates and faculty advisors,

My name is Richard Xue, and on behalf of the Secretariat team, it is my pleasure to extend a warm welcome to all of you attending the inaugural AlbertaMUN (ABMUN) conference, scheduled to take place on February 16th-17th, 2024. This year is particularly special as we embark on the first iteration of this conference, and we are excited to have you join us for this momentous occasion.

We have put significant thought and effort into crafting a diverse and engaging lineup of four exceptional committees. Each committee tackles a unique set of historical and contemporary issues, providing a wide range of topics for delegates to explore and deliberate upon. Whether you are an experienced MUN participant or a newcomer to the conference circuit, we have designed our committees to accommodate delegates of all skill levels, ensuring that everyone can make valuable contributions to the discussions.

The committee topics have been carefully selected to address pressing global challenges. From grappling issues from the mobilization of nuclear weapons, to facing a territorial dispute, to other important concerns, we aim to foster thought-provoking debates that deepen your understanding of international affairs. Our ultimate goal is to not only enhance your diplomatic and public speaking skills but also ignite a long-lasting passion for global issues.

As a Secretariat team, we are dedicated to making your ABMUN experience truly unforgettable. We will strive to provide you with a supportive and inclusive environment where you can learn, grow, and forge lasting connections with fellow delegates. We encourage you to seize this opportunity to engage with diverse perspectives, expand your horizons, and develop your leadership abilities. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us at abmun2024@gmail.com. We cannot wait for the first iteration of ABMUN.

Sincerely,

Richard Xue

Secretary General, ABMUN 2024
