Kennedy's Cabinet

Committee Description

Topic

In the heart of the Cold War, a high-stakes game of political brinkmanship unfolded, plunging the world into the depths of fear and uncertainty. It was a time when the fate of nations teetered on the edge of annihilation, and the smallest misstep could have ignited a global catastrophe. This is the Cuban Missile Crisis, an electrifying chapter in history that brought the United States and the Soviet Union to the precipice of nuclear war. As delegates, it is up to you to resolve this crisis through any means, either diplomatically or militarily.
