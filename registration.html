Registration

Overview

To participate in AlbertaMUN (ABMUN) conference, schools should register their delegation through our delegation registration form. Once registered, the school will be added to the delegate registration form within 3 days. While ABMUN encourages delegation participation, independent delegates are also welcome to register and join the conference. Registration starts on July 20, 2023. and ends on February 3, 2024


Delegate Fee: $15
